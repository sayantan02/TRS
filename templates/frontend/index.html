About Us

T.R.S is an independent, education nonprofit whose mission is to improve the practice of continuous learning, collaboration, and change in the education sector. Established in 2002, T.R.S conducts social science research, develops research-based innovations, and facilitates innovation that improves knowledge sharing in education.
